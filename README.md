# Wide Awake: A Mobile app that predicts home fire and notifies the elderly and carers when smoke/fire is detected
A fire prediction mobile app prototype that alerts the elderly when fire/smoke is detected. It notifies them and their carers to prevent deaths or fire-related injuries.

## Design Statement
To design a home fire prediction that alerts the elderly when fire/smoke is detected and sends notifications to them and their carers to prevent deaths or fire-related injuries.

## About the App
- It helps senior citizens to be FIRE-SAFE and FIRE-READY
- Serves as a warning alert system
- Prevents future fires
- Available on Android and iOS
